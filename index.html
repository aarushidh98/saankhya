behind Vastu

THE EMBLEM · Saa.nkhya

Three Forces
One Balance.

The Saankhya emblem encodes the three gunas of Samkhya philosophy. Each arc of the yantra corresponds to a force that shapes your environment — and your life.

Rajas
Movement · Fire · Creation
→
The force of kinetic energy. In Vastu practice, Rajas governs the south-east — the zone of fire, productivity and transformation. When balanced, it fuels purposeful action; in excess, it creates anxiety and conflict. Corrected through cooler tones, water elements, and open south-east walls.
Tamas
Grounding · Earth · Rest
→
The force of mass and structure. Tamas governs the south-west — the zone of earth, rest and emotional security. A home's master bedroom placed here grants deep, restorative sleep. Excess Tamas is remedied through light, decluttering, and open pathways.
Sattva
Clarity · Light · Wisdom
→
The force of luminous awareness. Sattva governs the north-east — the most sacred direction. Meditation rooms and altars placed here receive the highest blessings and cultivate clarity of mind. Keep this zone open, clean, and free of clutter.
